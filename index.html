Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

What is Mitto?+
Mitto is Riverstone Credit Union's global money transfer service, powered by PayMitto. It lets you send money to family and friends in 80+ countries directly from Riverstone digital banking — no third-party apps required.
How is it different from Zelle or Venmo?+
Zelle and Venmo are designed for domestic payments between people in the U.S. Mitto is built specifically for sending money internationally — handling currency conversion and global delivery to a bank account, debit card, or cash pickup location abroad.
Who can use Mitto?+
Mitto is available to Riverstone members with an eligible personal checking account who are enrolled in digital banking. Not a member yet? It only takes a few minutes to join.
How fast will my money arrive?+
Many transfers arrive within minutes. Bank deposits typically take 1–2 business days, while debit card and cash pickup are often available the same day. Exact timing depends on the destination and the recipient's bank.
What does it cost?+
You'll always see the transfer fee and exchange rate before you confirm — no hidden costs. For a limited time, Mitto transfers have a $0 transfer fee. Exchange rates include a small margin, shown upfront.
Are there transfer limits?+
Yes. For your protection, daily and per-transfer limits apply. You'll see your available limits in digital banking before you send.
What information do I need about my recipient?+
It depends on how they'd like to receive the money. For a bank deposit you'll need their account details; for a debit card, the card details; and for cash pickup, just their name as it appears on a valid ID.
Can I cancel a transfer?+
In many cases you can cancel a transfer within a short window after submitting it, as long as the funds haven't yet been deposited or collected. You'll find the option to cancel in digital banking.
How will I get confirmation?+
You'll receive a confirmation and a tracking view in digital banking the moment your transfer is on its way, so you can follow it from start to finish.
